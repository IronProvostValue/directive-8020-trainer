## ❓ FAQ
**Q: How does it differ from tools like PowerToys or AutoHotkey?**
A: PowerToys and AutoHotkey require manual scripting. Directive 8020 Trainer *learns* your habits and adapts automatically—no coding needed.

**Q: Is it safe?**
A: Yes. The `.exe` is signed, and all data stays on your machine. No internet access is required (unless you opt into optional update checks).

**Q: Can I use it at work?**
A: Check your IT policies. The app doesn’t modify system files, but some workplaces restrict automation tools.

**Q: What if I want to reset its learning?**
A: Head to `Settings > Advanced > Reset Training Data`. Your automations will revert to default, but your privacy settings stay intact.
